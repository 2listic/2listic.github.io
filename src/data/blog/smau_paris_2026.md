## Ciao Parigi!

It was a real pleasure to bring our vision to SMAU Paris and talk about how data centers can be managed more efficiently, an industry that keeps growing and becoming ever more strategic for technological progress.

![Dualistic team at SMAU Paris](/smau26/1.jpeg)

## Sharing our mission with Europe

Being hosted in the Italian Trade Agency startup area gave us the chance to meet investors, enablers, and fellow entrepreneurs from across Europe. The conversations at our booth confirmed what we have been seeing for months: the energy footprint of data centers is now a board level topic, and the industry is actively looking for concrete solutions rather than slides.

![Dualistic booth at SMAU Paris with the startup banner](/smau26/2.jpg)

That is exactly what DCIMate is about. Dualistic empowers data centers to slash energy use with DCIMate, an agentic AI system that autonomously optimizes power and cooling in real time. Instead of relying on static thresholds and reactive maintenance, DCIMate continuously observes the facility, reasons over its state, and acts, closing the loop between telemetry and operations.

## A live demo people could touch

We wanted visitors to leave with more than a pitch, so we brought our mascotte to the booth: meet **Decimino**, our miniature rack, a small cluster that let us show, live, how our agent perceives load, temperature, and power, and how it reacts when the conditions change.

![Decimino, the Dualistic minicluster mascotte](/smau26/3.jpg)

Seeing people stop, ask questions to Decimino's little fans, and immediately map the demo to their own infrastructure was the best feedback we could ask for.

## Thank you, and stay tuned

A huge thank you to SMAU for putting together such a well-curated event, and to everyone who visited us in Paris. The energy (pun intended) was exactly what a young company needs to push forward.

This is only one stop in a longer journey. Stay tuned, we will soon share where Dualistic is heading next.
